Soft Waste Recycling at Our
Spinning Mill

At Umar Textiles, sustainability starts before production begins. Each year, our spinning mill recycles over 1.2 tonnes of soft waste — clean cotton recovered from our own processes before it ever reaches the consumer.

Through our pre-consumer recycling initiative, we remove impurities from leftover cotton and return it to the yarn-making process, reducing waste and conserving valuable resources. This closed-loop system reflects our commitment to responsible manufacturing and a cleaner textile industry.

Hard Waste Recycling at Our
Spinning Mill

After the spinning process, all remaining waste including broken yarn and fibers that couldn’t be extracted is collected and recycled at our dedicated hard waste facility. This plant processes 4,000 kg of material daily, turning it back into usable cotton that’s reintroduced into production. Over the course of the year, this adds up to a significant volume of reclaimed fiber, reinforcing our commitment to sustainable, circular manufacturing.
