Umar Textiles is a state-of-the-art textile processing facility specializing in complete pretreatment and dyeing of textiles. We produce a wide range of towels and home textile products, crafted to meet the highest international standards.

Based in Karachi, the industrial hub of Pakistan, Umar Textiles has been delivering quality since 1998. Our products are trusted in markets around the world, from luxury retail to institutional and industrial use.

With a strong commitment to quality and innovation, we supply terry towels, barmops, institutional and industrial apparel, and textile accessories. Every product is tailored to the needs of our customers, whether for retail shelves or large-scale institutional requirements
